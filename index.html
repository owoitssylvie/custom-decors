Custom Avatar Decors

The largest collection of Avatar Decorations for the Decor Vencord Plugin.
You can download Vencord here. After Vencord is installed, go to Discord Settings -> Plugins and enable Decor!
You'll need to change the metadata of each decoration by reuploading to this site and then re-downloading.
